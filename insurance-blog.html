Why Insurance in 2025 Is Smarter and More Important Than Ever

Author: Sarah Lee | Date: March 3, 2025

Insurance is no longer just a safety net—it's now an intelligent, personalized financial tool. In 2025, AI-powered insurance platforms are making it easier to get coverage that fits your needs and budget.

  • AI pricing: Get fairer premiums based on your actual habits, not broad categories.

  • Bundled plans: Combine health, auto, and home for discounts.
